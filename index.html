HIT-ME

About-The- Game

HIT-ME   is   a  Java  GUI-based   game   with  three   levels,  enemy   appearances,  and   a  scoring   system.

How  It  Works

 In   HIT-ME ,  players  have  the  option  to  choose  different  levels  of  difficulty  to  challenge  themselves.  The  game  features  a  scoreboard  where  players an  view  their  current  score  and  track  their  progress  over  time.

 The  settings  menu  allows  players  to  customize  their  gaming  experience  by  adjusting  sound  settings  and  background  options  according  to  their  preferences. 

 In  each  level,  players  must  hit  the  monsters  within  a  given  amount  of  time  to  gain  points.  The  game  is  designed  to  provide  an  immersive  and  engaging  experience  for  players  of  all  ages. 

Beginner-Level

In this level, players have a time limit of 180 seconds to hit a target of 20 demons. However, they should be careful as they are allowed a maximum of 60 misses. The objective of this level is to hit the demons to gain points.

Intermediate-Level

In this level, players have 140 seconds to hit a target of 25 demons. They are allowed a maximum of 50 misses. The objective is to hit the demons to gain points (+1 point for each hit) and click on fire (-1 point for each hit) to proceed.

Expert-Level

In this level, players have 100 seconds to hit a target of 30 demons. They are allowed a maximum of 40 misses. The objective is to hit the demons to gain points (+1 point for each hit). However, clicking on "fire" will deduct 1 point. If the player clicks on "bomb", the game is over.
